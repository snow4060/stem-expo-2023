ABOUT

Ever wondered what our STEM clubs do every year, or if STEM is the right field for you?
At the STEM Expo, you'll get to see what each of Indy's STEM clubs and the STEAM academy have to offer through fun activities and demonstrations in an expo-style booth session.
There'll also be food, games, competitions, raffle prizes, free swag, and plenty of opportunities to connect with new people, so RSVP now!
Hope to see you there :)
