ABOUT

Ever wondered what our STEM clubs do every year, or if STEM is the right field for you?
At the STEM Expo, you'll get to see what each of Indy's STEM clubs and the STEAM academy have to offer through fun activities and demonstrations in an expo-style booth session.
There'll also be food, games, competitions, raffle prizes, free swag, and plenty of opportunities to connect with new people, so RSVP now!
Hope to see you there :)

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I register? Are there any fees?
Register here! No-- admission is completely free, and includes swag, food, and entry into our raffle.
How long is the Expo?
Around 2.5 hours! The Expo will be held from 3:30 PM to 6:00 PM.
Where is C-Commons?
C-Commons is located near the theater and staff parking lot towards the south end of campus. You can refer to this map of IHS.
What am I supposed to do at the Expo?
At the STEM Expo, you'll have the opportunity to learn about the STEM clubs and STEAM Academy classes we have at Independence. You should watch club and academy demonstrations and presentations, participate in club-organized activities at their booths, and listen to our panel of alumni who have gone on to study STEM at top universities while gaining industry experience.
What is a panel?
The STEM panel will be a group of IHS alumni in STEM who are going to speak in front of everyone regarding the STEM experience at IHS and beyond and answer your questions.
Will there be food?
Yes! Food will be served from 5:30 PM to 5:45 PM. You must have filled out our RSVP form to get food.
How does the raffle work?
You'll receive one raffle ticket upon RSVPing.
As of right now, the prizes are:
  • $25 Amazon gift card
  • Interactive plasma ball
  • Portable charger
  • Linux penguin plushie
  • 3D printed STEM Expo logo
  • Pins
Am I allowed to attend if I don't go to IHS?
Sure! However, you will not be able to get food or enter our raffle. Yes, we will be checking.
